PaidFair uses multi-validator AI consensus on GenLayer to determine what your profession is truly worth, across every market on earth. No surveys. No averages. Not one opinion. A network verdict.

The Method

Three steps. One cryptographically signed verdict.

No central authority. No proprietary survey. A panel of independent AI validators reaches consensus through GenLayer's optimistic protocol, then writes the verdict to chain.

01

Connect & submit

Connect your wallet, enter four fields — title, region, experience, and (optionally) your current salary. Your data is hashed before it leaves the device.

02

Validators deliberate

Multiple AI agents pull from labour-market sources, regional purchasing-power curves, and live posting data. Each reasons independently before reconciling.

03

Receive a signed verdict

Verdict, ranges by seniority, negotiation tactics, and a recommended floor — published with a transaction hash you can verify on-chain forever.
